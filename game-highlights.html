Game Highlights

Memorable Moments

The main highlights of this game are its story moments and cutscenes.Mainly the most praiseworthy story is definitely the latest update world penacony where the story was cleanly explained from different perspectives and showed us the motives of every character involved in the story.

Key Characters

1. Stelle/Caelus(MC)

Character Description

As Honkai Star Rail is a rpg(role-playing game),we will be introduced to the MC whom we will be following most of the time throughout our story.

At the beggining of the stroy we will be given to choose one of the MC and follow from his perspective,for now the origin of MC is yet to be revealed.


2.Bronya

Character Description

Bronya is a character who has an important role in the story of world Jurilo-VI(Belabog).

She is the current Supreme Guardian of Belobog,she is the young and capable commander of the Silvermane Guards. Bronya received rigorous education from an early age, and possesses the grace and affinity as expected of an heir.

Originally from Belobog's Underworld and the same orphanage as Seele, she was picked from a handful of children to become the next Supreme Guardian and adopted by Cocolia.


3.Sunday and Robin

Character Description

Sunday and Robin are twin siblings who will play an important role in the story of world Penacony,also known as "Planet of Festivities".

Sunday comes off as a dignified,benevolent and rational indvidual.He is reversed,seemingly determined and just.However,he is also prideful and cunning,willing to manipulate and lie in order to reach his goal of granting Robin's wish of a world where everyone can attain peace.

Meanwhile Robin is a elegant and demure young lady.A Halovian singer who was born in Penacony and has risen to cosmic fame.

Important Events

The most important event in any gacha game is obviously the Character event banners.

Event banners or in HSR terms known as Warp Banners is the place where the gacha system takes place i.e, where we can get the desired chracter we want by spending the ingame currency known as Stellar jades.

For instance, each version lasts for six weeks, and there are always two phases of the version, where each lasts for three weeks. When the three-week timer is over, players will see a change in the featured Warp Banner. Therefore, when a banner drops, Trailblazers have that much time to grind the game for every drop of Stellar Jade.
